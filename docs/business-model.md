# Business Model: Lab Support (Chemical & Physical Science Technicians)

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-3111`
- ISCO-08: `3111`
- Occupation: Chemical and Physical Science Technicians
- Social impact: lab-automation, quality-assurance, analytical-chemistry

## Customer

- analytical chemistry labs needing routine test protocol execution
- materials science labs needing sample analysis and calibration tracking
- QA/QC teams needing auditable result logging

## Offer

- registered test protocol execution on registered samples
- sample analysis result logging
- out-of-spec result flagging with human sign-off
- equipment calibration proposal and tracking

## Revenue

- per-sample analysis fee
- calibration/maintenance service retainer
- audit/compliance reporting fee

## Trust Controls

- no test protocol execution without a registered protocol and sample
- no equipment calibration without a registered equipment record
- out-of-spec results always escalate to human review, never silently logged
- committed records and the audit ledger are append-only, not editable
